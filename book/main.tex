\begin{abstract}
Starting from the sole assumption that \emph{points exist with pairwise relations}, we derive in sequence: the complex numbers $\mathbb{C}$ as the unique substrate, $d = 5$ as the unique dimension, the gauge group $\text{SU}(3)\times\text{SU}(2)\times\text{U}(1)$, all three coupling constants at $M_Z$, all 26 Standard Model parameters, and 9 cosmological observables. The theory has zero free parameters, zero observational inputs, and predicts the structure of its own errors via a trace-conservation sum rule.
\end{abstract}
