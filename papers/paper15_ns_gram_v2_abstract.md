# Paper 15 v2: 수정 방향

## 제목 (수정)
"Riccati Structure of the Navier-Stokes Nonlinearity
via Gram Matrix Decomposition"

## Abstract (수정)
We show that the nonlinear term of the 3D incompressible
Navier-Stokes equations, when expressed in terms of the
Gram matrix of normalized Fourier modes, has the structure
of a matrix Riccati equation dG/dt = -G² + R - νDG with
quadratic coefficient a = -1. This sign is an algebraic
consequence of the positive-semidefiniteness of G, independent
of the dynamics. For finite Galerkin truncations, this yields
global regularity. We identify the precise condition for
extending this to the continuum: that the cross-term residual R
satisfies a time-integrated bound relative to G².
