## Narrative

Nuclear magic numbers (2, 8, 20, 28, 50, 82, 126) emerge in DRLT
as atomic-substrate counts: shell-closure indices derivable from
the forced (NS, NT, d) primitive layer (read at presentation c = 2) +
spin-orbit corrections.

Binding energy per nucleon (~8 MeV plateau) derives from
cup-channel + Gram-self-energy machinery (same as α_em precision).
Deuteron binding (2.225 MeV) is the simplest case — two-nucleon
bound state.
