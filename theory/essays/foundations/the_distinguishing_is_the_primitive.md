# The distinguishing is the primitive ‚ÄĒ forced and necessary, machine-checked

**Triggering question.** The originator's correction: *rebuild from the **primitive distinguishing**,
not "from the residue"; the residue is what the distinguishing always leaves, and proving that is the
point.* So is "mathematics is the distinguishing's *forced* unfolding" substance or slogan ‚ÄĒ and what
is its **machine-checked** content?

## 213-native answer

The distinguishing ‚ÄĒ the act `Raw.slash (x y) (h : x ‚Č† y)`, the binary self-pointing of two
distinguishable atoms ‚ÄĒ is the one primitive. Everything the framework would call "metaphysics"
(the residue, no-exterior, infinity) is demoted to a *theorem about the act*. The substance of the
claim is a lattice of ‚ąÖ-axiom theorems with **two subjects** (arithmetic; the residue) and **four
predicates** (generation, forcing, reading-necessity, primitive-necessity). It is not one statement
to believe but a grid of checkable ones.

## Derivation ‚ÄĒ why the grid, not a slogan

"The distinguishing is the primitive" could be empty (anything can be *called* primitive). The grid
makes it non-empty by pinning four independently-falsifiable obligations and discharging each:

- **Generation** answers "does the act *produce* the structure?" `generation_capstone` routes ‚Ąē‚āä
  through the *canonical* `Lens.leaves` (not a bespoke projection ‚ÄĒ `value_eq_leaves`), so the
  positive natural `n+1` literally *is* the count-reading of the `n`-fold distinguishing. On the
  residue side, `lawvere_fixed_point` is the single construction `g a := t (f a a)` from which Cantor,
  the residue's own non-closure (at `A = Raw`), and the Liar/Tarski/Russell paradox all factor ‚ÄĒ the
  residue is the *engine* of the limitative theorems, not four separate facts.
- **Forcing** answers "is the structure *forced* or merely *consistent*?" `count_reading_forced` is
  initiality with teeth: fixing the interpretation (atom‚Ü¶1, slash‚Ü¶+) leaves *no freedom* ‚ÄĒ the
  reading is unique. `distinguishing_always_leaves_residue` is the dual on the residue: the self-cover
  is faithful yet never total, so a remainder is *forced*, not posited.
- **Reading-necessity** answers "could a *different* reading do as well?" No: a distinguishing-blind
  combine (`fun _ _ => 1`) collapses arithmetic (`distinguishing_necessary`), and a subsingleton
  value-space kills the diagonal (`residue_needs_distinguishing`). Only readings that *use* the
  distinction generate anything.
- **Primitive-necessity** answers the skeptic's sharpest form ‚ÄĒ "could a *different primitive* do as
  well?" The distinguishing is in the **type** of `slash` (`x ‚Č† y` is a hypothesis of the
  constructor). On a carrier that draws no distinction, the primitive cannot fire *even once*
  (`no_distinguishing_on_subsingleton`); Raw meets the precondition (`a ‚Č† b`,
  `raw_has_distinguishing`). So a non-distinguishing rival is not a slower route to the same place ‚ÄĒ
  it has *no* route.

## Dual function ‚ÄĒ the classical reading

This is the old dream of grounding mathematics on *distinction* (Spencer-Brown's "draw a
distinction"; Brouwer's two-oneness; the Lawvere‚ÄďYanofsky diagonal as the one self-reference engine)
‚ÄĒ with the one thing those programmes never supplied: a **machine-checked, zero-axiom** discharge of
the unfolding. The grid above is that discharge for two domains. What is *new* is not the philosophy
(old) but the proof obligation met: `#print axioms` empty on each cell.

## Honest scope (the self-check, and what is NOT claimed)

- **`‚ąÖ-axiom` ‚Č† "from one primitive."** It means free of `propext`/`Classical.choice`/`Quot.sound`/
  Mathlib ‚ÄĒ *not* free of the kernel's inductives, ő†-types, and `Bool`, which are the ambient
  apparatus the grid runs on. The honest claim is "the act forces the structure *within* that
  apparatus," not "the act is the sole ground of logic."
- **Primitive-necessity is the degenerate corner, not the full theorem.** `no_distinguishing_on_subsingleton`
  excludes a *non-distinguishing* rival. Structured rivals (negation-first, relation-first) generating
  a *different/degenerate* arithmetic are the open middle (`frontiers/the_descent_leg.md`).
- **The foundational thesis is untestable from inside** (`05_no_exterior.md` ¬ß5.1) ‚ÄĒ the grid is
  internal evidence; external exposure (the formalization paper) stays the rank-1 frontier
  (`the_substance_test.md`).
- **No view promoted to identity.** This essay is a Lens on the residue and re-objectifies on reading
  ‚ÄĒ `object1_not_surjective` says no view (including this grid) encloses it. The grid is *facets made
  checkable*, not the thing itself.
