# Non-holonomicity as escaping the finite-state machine

**Triggering question.**  A real's continued fraction `(aᵢ)` is *non-holonomic* when no linear
recurrence with polynomial coefficients governs it — the conjectured status of π.  What does
that *mean* structurally, why is it the residue's own signature read one scale up, and where is
its practical edge?

## The claim

> **Holonomic = generated by a finite-state machine (bounded memory).  Non-holonomic = a trace
> that escapes every finite-state machine.**  π's (conjectured) continued-fraction
> non-holonomicity is the residue's *source-without-enclosure* — `CoResidue.spineL_escapes` — read
> at the continued-fraction scale.

A finite linear recurrence emits the next term from a finite window: bounded memory, a
finite-state transition.  Two machine classes name the two natural finite transitions, and the
residue escapes their union.

## Derivation by citation

**The foundational shape.**  Read as an automaton, the self-pointing residue is the
free-running counter trace `spineL`: a genuine behaviour (`CoResidue.spineL_antiRefl`) that is
the trace of **no finite reachable state** (`CoResidue.spineL_escapes`,
`FlatOntologyClosure.object1_not_surjective`).  The finite carrier `Raw` reads as a *reachable,
reduced, deterministic* machine (`StateMachine.mu_carrier_reachable_reduced_machine`); exactly
one behaviour — the counter — escapes it (`StateMachine.counter_no_return`,
`residue_escapes_minimal_machine`).  "Escapes every finite-state machine" is the residue's
defining move.

**The two machine classes, at the sequence scale.**  Over `ℤ` — the readout group in which the
difference `Δ s n = s(n+1) − s n` closes (`integers_as_difference_lens.md`) — the bounded-memory
sequences split:

  - **time-varying, linear** — `ZeroRunNonHolonomic.HomogRec`: a homogeneous recurrence
    `lead(n)·a(n+k) = R(n, window)` with `lead` eventually nonzero (every integer P-recursive
    sequence satisfies one).  Escape certificate: `zero_run_not_homogRec` — a sequence with
    arbitrarily long zero-runs and infinitely many nonzero terms satisfies none (a zero window
    forces, via `lead ≠ 0`, every later term to zero).
  - **time-invariant, arbitrary** — `ZeroRunNonHolonomic.AutoRec`: a fixed window-rule `F`,
    `n`-independent — the classical deterministic finite-state machine.  Escape certificate:
    `two_continuations_not_autoRec` — a window pattern with two distinct continuations is
    emitted by no such machine (the Myhill–Nerode kernel: one state cannot yield two outputs).

**The witnesses.**  The growth direction: `(n!)ⁿ` outruns every bounded-memory envelope
(`NonHolonomicWitness.superFact_nonHolonomic`, the elementary Klazar bound).  The bounded,
sparse direction: the indicator of the powers of two, `χ`, is non-holonomic in **both** senses
at once — `ZeroRunNonHolonomicWitness.chi_nonHolonomic` (`¬ HomogRec χ`) and
`chi_not_autoRec` (`¬ AutoRec χ`, since an all-zero length-`k` window is continued by `0`
mid-run and by `1` just before a power of two).  `χ` is bounded yet needs unbounded memory:
the powers-of-two trace escapes every finite-state machine, the CF-scale shadow of
`spineL_escapes`.

**Why the tier is a ring stratification.**  The finite-Δ-depth `ℤ`-sequences form a ring
(`FiniteDepthAlgebra.polyDepthZ_mul`: depths add under product) with exact normal form
(`NewtonGregory.reconstruct`: a depth-`d` sequence *is* `Σ_{i≤d}(Δⁱs 0)·binom(·,i)`) and
polynomial growth bound (`NewtonGregory.poly_bound`).  The holonomicity tier measures where a
partial-quotient sequence sits relative to this generating ring; the genuine non-holonomic tier
is outside it — no finite generating machine.

## Dual function

Classically this is the **holonomic / P-recursive** dividing line and its automata-theoretic
twin (a C-finite sequence over `GF(2)` *is* a linear-feedback shift register; a deterministic
finite automaton cannot emit two outputs from one state — Myhill–Nerode).  The 213 reading
sharpens *what* is escaping (the residue) and *which structure* it escapes (the difference-Lens
generating ring), and grounds the slogan **"holonomicity is a property of the pointing, not the
number"** (`PresentationDependence.crossDetSmall_is_presentation_dependent`, `rcut_rescale`): a
pointing *is* a choice of machine for the same residue; the residue is the behaviour all of them
converge to and none generates as a finite state.

## Cross-frame

One fact in four frames: the residue's `spineL_escapes` (foundational) · `chi_not_autoRec`
(automata / FSM) · `chi_nonHolonomic` (P-recursive / D-finite) · `poly_bound` outside the
finite-depth ring (difference calculus).  The µF/νF pair — reachable finite states vs the
escaping trace — is not a dichotomy but one self-pointing act read at its two cofinal ends.

## The practical edge

The line *has a finite generating machine* / *does not* is where computation lives.

  - **Computing π = choosing a holonomic pointing.**  Record computations use the Chudnovsky
    hypergeometric series — a holonomic pointing admitting binary splitting — because the
    continued fraction, being non-holonomic, is useless for fast computation.  Same number, two
    pointings; one computes through the holonomic one.  This is the working principle of
    creative telescoping / the holonomic systems approach: holonomic objects have finite
    descriptions a computer-algebra system can store, sum, and certify.  (Classical, cited.)
  - **Sequence generation and a usable test.**  C-finite over `GF(2)` = an LFSR (CRC, stream
    ciphers, PRNGs); the non-holonomic witnesses admit no such finite recurrence.
    `zero_run_not_homogRec` is a ready criterion — *arbitrarily long zero-runs + infinitely many
    nonzero ⟹ no homogeneous linear recurrence* — so a "guess-the-recurrence" search can be
    abandoned with proof that none exists.
  - **Interpolation.**  `NewtonGregory` is the exact forward-difference calculus — Newton
    forward-difference interpolation and Newton–Cotes integration; a data sequence is degree-`d`
    polynomial-interpolatable iff its difference table bottoms out at depth `d`.

## Constructive accessibility

The essay lands on syntactic objects: `chi_not_autoRec : ¬ AutoRec χ` and
`chi_nonHolonomic : ¬ HomogRec χ` — `χ = ` the indicator of the powers of two, a total
computable `0/1` function (`chi_values`), proven to escape both finite-state machine classes.

## Open frontier

  - π's *own* continued-fraction non-holonomicity is classically open and not closable
    ∅-axiom: the analytic shape obstruction (Flajolet–Gerhold–Salvy) has no constructive shadow
    and bottoms out at the unproven Gauss–Kuzmin normality of π.  The escape is *certified* here
    only for constructed witnesses, not for π.
  - A third machine class — the mod-2 forbidden-factor obstruction (Garrabrant–Pak) — is
    ∅-axiom-reachable in principle (elementary 2-adic valuation) but needs valuation
    infrastructure not yet built; it too is vacuous on π (conjecturally generic).
  - "Holonomic ⟹ generated by a finite-state machine" is read here through the two named classes
    (`HomogRec`, `AutoRec`); a single unified machine predicate subsuming both is not formalised.
