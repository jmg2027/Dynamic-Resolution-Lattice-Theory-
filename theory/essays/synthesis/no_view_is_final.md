# No view is final — degree relativity without relativism

Readings differ by the layer they are taken from, and the search across layers
does not terminate.  This is not "everything is relative": the views differ, but
what they are views *of* does not reduce to them, and that residue is what makes
the search endless rather than arbitrary.

## 213-native answer

A reading is a **Lens-output of the pointing**, not a property of the thing
pointed at.  The degree of a real is the growth class of the receipt *a pointing*
carries (`theory/essays/synthesis/finite_state_is_of_the_pointing.md`;
`theory/essays/analysis/the_degree_of_a_number.md`): change the presentation, change
the degree.  So "it differs by view" is exact and theorem-backed —
`PresentationDependence` makes holonomicity a property of the approximant sequence,
and `Real213/Modulus/DegreeCriterion` fixes the degree two-sidedly from the
pointing's own data (`⌊i^{1/s}⌋·W_i` against the denominator increment), with
nothing of a view-independent "value" entering.

## Derivation

Two facts keep this from collapsing into relativism.

First, **no view is final**.  The residue is outside every view's image
(`Theory/Raw/CoResidue.object1_not_surjective`): no cover surjects onto it, so
there is always a further reading.  The modulus-degree ladder makes this concrete
and unbounded — `Real213/Modulus/RateHierarchy.strict_modulus_hierarchy` separates
every consecutive rung `(t, t+1)` by an explicit presentation, so there is no top
rung, no last schedule, no view at which the search could stop.  The endlessness
is not a failure to finish; it is the form of a source without enclosure
(`theory/essays/foundations/the_form_of_the_residue.md`,
`seed/AXIOM/05_no_exterior.md` §5.1) — readings flow *out*, nothing reads *back* to
close it (`theory/essays/foundations/reached_by_none.md`).

Second, **something survives the change of view**.  The real is the invariant
under rescheduling (`reschedule_limit_eq`): the degree is a Lens-output, but the
cut it points at is fixed across all the schedules that read it.  So the differing
readings are facets of one residue, and the residue does not reduce to any facet —
exactly the "View promoted to identity" guard (CLAUDE.md failure catalog): a
reading is a facet, not the thing.

Searching out as many readings as can be found is then the activity itself, not a
preamble to a final answer: because `object1_not_surjective` guarantees no reading
is last, the breadth of readings *is* the work — which is precisely the §7.1
reading of primacy as breadth (`seed/AXIOM/07_primacy.md`), the residue reproduced
domain after domain.

## Dual function

Stripped of packaging, this is the constructive stance — there is no view-free
object to adjudicate "which reading is real," only readings with receipts — with
one classical reflex removed: the reflex to then conclude "so nothing is real."
213's sharpening is to keep the residue as the un-enclosed invariant (the cut under
rescheduling, the `1 = det P` shadow), so relativity of the *reading* coexists with
invariance of the *residue* — facet-relativity, not relativism.

## Cross-frame connections

The same fact in four resolutions: no-exterior (`05_no_exterior.md` §5.1 — no
outside view to be final), the cover non-surjection
(`object1_not_surjective` — no reading captures the residue), the unbounded ladder
(`strict_modulus_hierarchy` — no top rung), and the rescheduling invariant
(`reschedule_limit_eq` — the residue survives every view).  "There is no last view"
and "there is an invariant" are the un-enclosed-source read forward (out) and
backward (no back).

## Open frontier

Whether the invariant a search converges on is itself decidable for a given object
is, in the sharpest case (the irrationality-measure threshold cut), Roth-grade and
unclaimed (`theory/essays/analysis/the_degree_of_a_number.md` open frontier).  The
breadth claim — that the same residue-form recurs across domains — is a program
measured by how far it is carried, not a finished theorem.

## Self-check note

Retreat caught in-place: "everything differs by view" invites the import
*"therefore all readings are equally arbitrary."*  That meta-claim is itself an
exterior, view-independent assertion — the very thing no-exterior denies — and it
promotes one reading ("relativity") to the identity of the residue.  The
correction is not to pick a privileged view but to keep the residue un-enclosed:
readings differ (`PresentationDependence`), the residue does not reduce to them
(`reschedule_limit_eq`, `object1_not_surjective`).  The differing is structured, not
arbitrary.
