# What 213 forces, and what it reads

213 forces a small mathematical skeleton — a few numbers, some counting,
the cohomology of one lattice — and our universe's physics is *read off*
that skeleton.  The forcing is genuine (no free parameter); the physical
names are readings, not forced.

## 213-native answer

Pointing leaves a residue; distinguishing is the primitive act
(`seed/AXIOM/01_residue.md`).  Everything 213 *forces* is a property of that
act: how many you need to distinguish (`NT = 2`), what the residue of
distinguishing yields (`NS = 3`), their atomic closure (`d = NS + NT = 5`) —
uniquely (`Theory/Atomicity/PairForcing`, `Five`, `NonDecomposable`).  From
these, more numbers fall out by counting alone: the cohomology rank
`NS² − 1 = 8` (`Physics/Symmetry/GluonChannelInterpretation.eight_fold_QCD_identification`,
the rank `b₁(H¹K) = 8 = NS²−1`), the generation count
`C(NS, NT) = 3` (`Physics/Simplex/Generations`), the Basel truncation depths
`{1, 2, ∞}` by Gram-sector rank exhaustion
(`Physics/Cosmology/NeffDerivation`, `basel_formula_axiom_derived`).  These are
∅-axiom, `decide`-true, parameter-free.

What 213 does **not** force is the *identification* of those numbers with
physical objects.  "`8` = the SU(3) gluon octet", "`Δ⁴` = spacetime",
"`6π⁵` = the proton/electron ratio" — each is a reading laid on a forced
number, carried in prose or a defined projection, never derived.  The forced
`(F₂)⁸` cohomology is a characteristic-2 vector space; the SU(3) adjoint is a
real Lie algebra; the match is the *number* `8`, not the object
(`Physics/Symmetry/OctetModule`, the rank-8 𝔽₂ carrier).  Even where a reading is pushed toward
forced — the octet read as a cokernel of the zero map out of a contractible
`Δ⁴`, the CP phase's `C₄` read as the order of a `90°` matrix — the math
closes (often only *to a model*) while the physical name stays a reading.

## Dual function

Physics is usually conceived as laws *out there* that mathematics describes.
Strip the exterior-laws packaging — there is no outside to distinguishing
(`seed/AXIOM/05_no_exterior.md` §5.1), and every measurement *is* a
distinguishing — and physics becomes the self-consistent structure of
distinguishing itself.  That is the interpretation.  The refinement 213 adds
is sharp: only the *counting* is forced (the numbers, ∅-axiom, zero dials);
the named physical objects are readings, and freezing a reading into "this
number IS that particle" is exactly the meaning-fixing the residue forbids
(`seed/AXIOM/06_lens_readings.md`; the meta-principle).  So "Raw derives
physics" means: Raw forces the skeleton our universe's numbers exhibit; the
dictionary onto our specific particles is interpretation.

## Cross-frame connections

The forced/read split is the residue/Lens split at the physics scale.  The
forced numbers are properties of the distinguishing act; the readings are
Lens-outputs that no single view exhausts
(`Lens/FlatOntologyClosure.object1_not_surjective`).  It is the physics form
of "no view is final" (`theory/essays/synthesis/no_view_is_final.md`):
readings differ, the residue reduces to none.  The genuinely zero-dial content
is small — on the order of three logically independent measured numbers
(`N_gen = 3`; the rank-8 colour structure `NS² − 1`; the Koide ratio
`NT/NS = 2/3`, `Physics/Foundations/KoideFormula`) — yet pinned with no
parameter, plus the over-determination that one forced atom set must produce
three gauge-coupling skeletons at three *forced* depths and cannot be tuned to
fit one without the others.  The continuous side (the `π` in the couplings, the
precision digits) is the never-closing modulus of the *same* discrete pointing
— bracketed to any precision by a discrete approximant, reached by no finite
stage (`Real213/ExpLog/PiCut`, `Real213/AbCutSeq`; "infinity is the residue's
shape", `theory/essays/foundations/the_form_of_the_residue.md`).  So even the
continuous match is a discrete bracket, not an exterior real handed in.

## Open frontier

How far the readings close to forced is not settled — it is *measured* by the
trajectory of converting readings into math.  Where the forcing genuinely
reaches an SU(3) *group* (not merely the dimension `8`), a Hodge `⋆`-operator
acting on real cochains (`Cohomology/Hodge/Star`) rather than a matrix labelled
`⋆`, a *constructed* continuous value rather than a bracket of a hypothesised
limit — these are open.  The honest count of independent zero-dial matches is
itself small and debatable, and the headline tally of "observables" inflates it
several-fold by re-reading the same handful of forced integers across domains.

## Self-check note

The temptation is to over-state the closures — to call the octet or the CP
phase "genuinely derived".  Held against the Lean, both close the *math* only
to a model (a `Unit`-modelled trivial cohomology; a matrix identified with the
Hodge star by docstring, not by an equation) while the physical name stays a
reading.  The honest statement is the forced skeleton plus the read dictionary,
with the genuine parameter-free content being the small set of independent
counts — not the precision digits, which ride a literal `π` the coupling files
feed in.
