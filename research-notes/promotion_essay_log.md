row.  Capture the *situation*, not just the artifact: what had just
happened that made promotion/essay feel right at that moment.  Newest at

|---|------|------|----------------|--------------------------------------------------------|----------------|

- **Type** вҖ” `promotion` (Lean sub-tree вҶ’ `theory/` chapter) or `essay`
