## The decomposition (C / Reading / Residue)

- **Construction `C`** — distinguishing, iterated: the free inductive type itself. The calculus's
  `Raw` (`Lens/LensCore.lean`, built on `Tree`) is the **term model** — the free type generated by two
  bases (`Raw.a`, `Raw.b`) and one combine rule (`Raw.slash`). A **proof** is a construction: a `Raw`-tree
  built by the proof-rules. A **term** of a type is the same tree. So `C` = proof = term = the
  distinguishing-history, before any reading is chosen. There is no proof-object distinct from a
  term-object; they are one construction.

- **Reading `L`** — the **type-as-reading**: a proposition `A` is a `Lens` — a feature the construction
  is projected to (`Raw → α`, `Lens.view`). To say "`p` is a proof of `A`" is to say the construction `p`
  *inhabits* the reading `A` — it is read through `A` and lands in `A`'s value space. Three derived
  readings:
  - **a proposition `A` = a type = a reading `L`** — the value space the construction is projected into.
  - **implication `A → B`** = the **function / Lens-arrow** `Lens.refines` (`LensCore.lean:90`): a reading
    *between* readings, "everything the `A`-reading separates, the `B`-reading also separates." A proof of
    `A → B` is a refinement of one reading by another.
  - **β-reduction / cut-elimination** = the **reduction-reading**: evaluate the recursor, peel a
    redex/cut, descend toward normal form. This reading's well-founded floor is the normal form.

- **Residue** — the reduction-reading's self-application surplus, splitting at the two `q=±1` poles (the
  load-bearing find, the proof-theory companion-split to `godel.md`):
  - **`q=+1` (converge / terminate) — NORMALIZATION & CONSISTENCY.** When the reduction-reading
    **closes** on a fixed point: β-reduction converges to the **normal form** — the unique canonical
    inhabitant (`dhom_unique_pointwise`: the only arrow out of the free object is forced). Strong
    normalization = the reduction reading TERMINATES (`no_infinite_descent` / `isPart_wf`: every descent
    chain bottoms out at the atomic floor — the well-founded floor IS the normal form). Consistency = no
    proof of `⊥` = the well-founded floor reached, the converging unimodular pole (`ResidueTag` `q=+1`,
    `converge_residue_fixed`, the same `q=+1` as φ/Gaussian/ODE).
  - **`q=−1` (escape / reached-by-none) — GÖDEL / the diagonal.** The provability self-cover re-enters
    itself (`godel.md`): the diagonal sentence "I am not provable" has no row — fixed-point-free, the
    escaping pole. Consistency-cannot-prove-its-own-consistency is the `q=−1` escape of the *same*
    self-cover whose `q=+1` face is normalization's termination.

```
   a proof p              =  a construction (a Raw-tree built by the proof-rules)         (C)
   a proposition A        =  a type = a reading (a Lens, Raw → α)                         (L)
   "p proves A"           =  ⟨ p | A ⟩ = the construction p inhabits the reading A        (OBJECT = ⟨C|L⟩)
   CURRY–HOWARD           =  the calculus's ⟨Construction | Reading⟩ IS ⟨proof | proposition⟩
                            (the term model = Raw = the free inductive type; a Lens = a type-as-reading)
   implication A → B      =  the Lens-arrow Lens.refines (a reading between readings)
   modus ponens           =  composition refines_trans  (A→B and B→C give A→C; cut on B)
   the λ ↔ ⊢ correspondence =  view = fold  (model_theory.md's completeness = initiality, SAME theorem:
                                the syntactic/free side Raw.fold and the semantic/universal side Lens.view
                                are ONE unique arrow out of the initial object)
   NORMALIZATION          =  Residue(reduction-reading, C) at q=+1 = the fold to the normal form
                             (raw_initial + dhom_unique_pointwise: the normal form is the UNIQUE
                              canonical inhabitant — the unique arrow out of the initial/free object)
   STRONG NORMALIZATION   =  Residue(reduction-reading, C) at q=+1 = the reduction TERMINATES
                             (no_infinite_descent / isPart_wf: no infinite reduction chain; every
                              descent bottoms out at the atomic floor = the well-founded normal form)
   CONSISTENCY            =  the q=+1 well-founded floor reached (no proof of ⊥)
   GÖDEL                  =  Residue(provability-reading, C) at q=−1 = the escaping diagonal (godel.md)
```

So Curry–Howard is not a new field to import — it is the calculus's own founding sentence
`OBJECT = ⟨C | L⟩` read with `C` = proof = term and `L` = proposition = type. The correspondence is the
calculus recognizing that its substrate (`Raw` a free inductive type, `Lens.view` a recursor) **is** a
type theory.

## THE REVELATION — Curry–Howard is the calculus's own ⟨C|L⟩ = ⟨proof | proposition⟩; normalization = the fold; strong normalization = the q=+1 well-founded floor

**Curry–Howard is the calculus reading its own type-theoretic substrate back to itself — a near
fixed-point, like `yoneda.md`.** The calculus did not adopt propositions-as-types; the calculus's
`Raw` *is* the free inductive type (the term model), `Lens.view = Raw.fold` *is* the recursor, and a
`Lens` *is* a type-as-reading. So the Curry–Howard slogans land term-for-term on already-proven ∅-axiom
objects:

- **propositions = types = readings; proofs = terms = constructions** = `OBJECT = ⟨C | L⟩` — the
  README's normal form, with `C` the proof/term and `L` the proposition/type. A proof of `A` is a
  construction inhabiting the reading `A`. No proof-object behind the term; they are one `Raw`.

- **the λ ↔ ⊢ correspondence = `view = fold` = `model_theory.md`'s completeness = the SAME initiality
  theorem.** Curry–Howard says: the syntactic side (a λ-term / a natural-deduction derivation, built
  freely) and the semantic side (its denotation / what it proves) are the *same object*. In the calculus
  the free/syntactic side is `Raw.fold` (`Fold.lean:22`) and the universal/semantic side is `Lens.view`
  (`LensCore.lean:42`); they are **the same unique arrow** out of the initial object `Raw`
  (`raw_initial`, `SemanticAtom.lean:412`; `universalMorphism_unique`, `:388`; `dhom_unique_pointwise`,
  `UniversalDistinguishing.lean:103`). This is *exactly* the theorem `model_theory.md` uses for
  completeness `⊢φ ⟺ ⊨φ` — Curry–Howard and completeness are the **same `view=fold` initiality**, read
  once as proofs-as-programs and once as syntax-meets-semantics.

- **★ NORMALIZATION = the fold to the unique normal form = `view=fold` initiality.** Cut-elimination /
  β-reduction reduces a proof to its normal form. In the calculus, evaluating the recursor (`Raw.fold`)
  *is* the unique map out of the initial object, and `dhom_unique_pointwise` proves any two such maps
  agree pointwise — the normal form is the **unique canonical inhabitant**, pinned by initiality. So
  "reduce to normal form" = "evaluate the catamorphism" = "the unique arrow out of `Raw`." Normalization
  is not a separate procedure; it is `view = fold` read as a reduction. (This is the proof-theory face
  of `model_theory.md`'s completeness and `yoneda.md`'s "the arrow out of the representable is pinned by
  one datum" — three readings of `dhom_unique_pointwise`.)

- **★ STRONG NORMALIZATION = `no_infinite_descent`'s `q=+1` well-founded termination [BUILT, PURE].**
  This is the leverage — both sides are PURE-built. Strong normalization is the statement that there is
  *no infinite reduction sequence* — every reduction chain terminates. The calculus proves exactly this
  for its peel/descent reading: `Lambek.no_infinite_descent` (`Theory/Raw/Lambek.lean:273`, PURE) — there
  is **no** total `chain : Nat → Raw` peeling at every step (a chain of length `depth+1` would drive
  depth below zero); equivalently `Lambek.isPart_wf` (`:199`, PURE) — the peel relation `IsPart` is
  **well-founded**, every descent bottoms out at the **atomic floor** (`terminal_iff_atom`) in finitely
  many steps, with the explicit rate `descent_chain_drops` (`:252`: each peel removes at least the unit
  `1` of depth). The **atomic floor IS the normal form** — the terminal construction nothing reduces
  further (`no_part_of_atom`). And `MuNuMirror.ascent_total_descent_partial` (`:105`, PURE) is the exact
  asymmetry proof theory needs: the *ascent* (building proofs upward) has an explicit total stream
  (`tower_ascent_isPart`), but the *descent* (reduction) has **no** infinite chain — strong
  normalization is the universal-negation half. The `succ_not_idempotent` (`:80`, PURE) growing pole is
  the iteration-character's reduction-relevant face: reduction strictly drops depth, so it cannot cycle.

- **★ CONSISTENCY `q=+1` vs GÖDEL `q=−1` — one residue at two signs on the proof-self-cover.**
  Consistency = "no proof of `⊥`" = the reduction-reading reaches its **well-founded floor**, the
  converging unimodular pole (`ResidueTag` `q=+1`, `converge_residue_fixed`, `ResidueTag.lean:160`,
  PURE — the same `q=+1` engine as φ via `golden_is_converge`, `:180`). Strong normalization → consistency
  is the classic proof-theory move (a normal proof of `⊥` would be a direct contradiction, impossible),
  and it is the calculus's `q=+1` termination. Gödel's "consistency cannot be proved internally" is the
  `q=−1` escape of the *same* proof-self-cover (`godel.md`: `russell_liar_no_surjection`, the
  fixed-point-free diagonal). So normalization's termination (`q=+1`, `no_infinite_descent`) and Gödel's
  diagonal (`q=−1`, `object1_not_surjective`) are **one residue at its two unimodular signs** on the
  proofs-as-constructions reading — the `ResidueTag` `residue_tag_two_poles` (`:228`, PURE) exhibited on
  proof theory, the proof-theoretic companion to `model_theory.md`'s completeness/incompleteness split.

**Net revelation:** Curry–Howard consolidates FIVE prior decompositions under the two invariants. It is
`category_theory.md`'s self-description (the calculus IS a type theory), `model_theory.md`'s `view=fold`

This is the *exact* analogue of `model_theory.md`'s absent FOL syntax/satisfaction/proof-system and
