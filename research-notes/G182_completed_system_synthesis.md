## The one claim

213's deepest purpose: stop crossing the "hard sea" of abstract mathematics by the
intermittent lightning of individual genius, and instead work **on a completed structure** —
not to *eliminate* the frontier, but to give even the frontier a **definite form**, so that
talent is spent *navigating known coordinates* rather than guessing in the dark.

The structural reason this is possible is the **µF/νF mirror**, and the structural reason it
does not collapse into "nothing is left to do" is that the frontier (νF) is *complete as a
coinductive object*, never as a finite one.

## The spine — µF/νF (PROVEN structure)

The self-pointing act `F` has two fixed points (`Theory/Raw/MuNuMirror`,
`Theory/Raw/Lambek`, `Lens/ResidueReentry`, `Cauchy/DepthCeilingResidue`):

  - **µF = Raw** — the descent face: well-founded, terminal at atoms, **inductive-complete**.
    *Crankable*: every reading flows out of it (`raw_initial`); descent always bottoms out
    (`no_infinite_descent`, `terminal_iff_atom`).
  - **νF = the residue** — the ascent face: non-well-founded, the self-cover surplus,
    **coinductive-complete** (`CoResidue`/`CoShape` = the final coalgebra: weakly final +
    faithful embedding `object1_injective`; `object1_not_surjective` = the surplus).
    *Not crankable, but definite*: a unique, precisely-shaped object, explored coinductively.

So the dichotomy is **not** "structured floor vs formless sea."  It is
**inductive-complete vs coinductive-complete** — both are completed forms.

## The honest boundary (why genius is still needed — the good outcome)

A complete *form* does not decide every *fact*: a coinductive object can carry undecidable
properties.  So the frontier's **map** is complete (νF's shape is definite), while particular
**summits** stay unreached — e.g. "is π in this νF-class?" = π non-holonomic, open
(`research-notes/G170`).  This is completion, not deficiency: talent moves from *map-less
navigation* to *summiting on coordinates*.  The frontier recedes forever (νF is unbounded,
`ascent_unbounded`) and stays *mapped* — so there is always a place to climb, with a compass.

## What is PROVEN vs PROGRAM (the standing honesty guard)

  - **Proven (∅-axiom)**: µF/νF as definite objects; Raw initial; the residue non-surjective;
    the `+1` unit's cross-domain identity; the `HurwitzianCF` tiers; the spiral/Markov/modular
