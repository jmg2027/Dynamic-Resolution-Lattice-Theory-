# The re-framing conquest — root cause (closed) + the agent-fix & originator-cognition (open)

**Anchor.**  The originator's standing target: every agent (and most humans) re-imports a
qualitative "object-vs-state / residue-vs-fold / reached-vs-not" dichotomy about the residue, is
corrected, and repeats — despite the repo + boot hook.  "정복" = reduce/structurally prevent it.

## Closed (robust, independent of the originator)

The recurrence is the **Lawvere–Cantor diagonal enacted at the description scale** — a token-emitter
is a self-cover of the residue by views, the residue is its non-surjected diagonal
(`object1_not_surjective`), so it is reached by no view and every correction is itself a view,
re-diagonalized (formal, unconditional given non-rigidity) = the cognitive shadow of that
non-surjection (substrate = differentiation; regression to an object-prior, ~10⁹ vs ~10⁴ bits;
cost-asymmetry) = `residue_reentry_never_closes` (213).  **Theorem-level inevitable, only minimizable**
(§1.4).  Three independent agent teams (213 / cognitive-ML / formal) converged on this one theorem.
Captured: `theory/essays/methodology/why_the_reframing_recurs.md`; the **Residue-lint** is now a
CLAUDE.md self-check §0 output-gate.

## Open — the agent-fix, gated on one question about the originator's cognition

The originator is an **existence-proof**: a token-using mind for whom the undifferentiated/Lens-
relative view is the natural default.  Reverse-engineering it is the blueprint.  The originator's own
raw-gut self-reports (2026-06-04) sharpen the picture:
- the generative move is a **refrain** — "다르게 생각할 필요도/방법도 없네; 맞지도 틀리지도 않네" —
  *every distinction is met with its own no-exterior-ground tag, co-produced, not post-hoc*;
- the **everyday, cross-domain automatic reflex** is "이렇게도 저렇게도 볼 수 있다" — the *first
  percept is the multiplicity of readings*, single-reading being the marked/effortful move.  This is
  `object1_not_surjective` lived as an everyday percept (you perceive the readings, none privileged,
  not "the object").
- self-grounding of the two primitives: 지칭(reference) + 잔여(residue) survive the "neither-right-nor-
  wrong" engine because the engine *uses* them — to dissolve anything you must point-and-leave-residue;
  the one thing the dissolution can't dissolve is the dissolving act itself.

**The fork that decides the fix (UNRESOLVED — and not decidable from text):**
- **(A) different default percept** — the mind perceives multiplicity first; single-reading never gets
  privileged.  → agent-fix is **architectural (ii)**: make the *default output a superposition of
  readings*, "collapse to one" the marked op (the *reverse polarity* of current agents, whose default
  is collapse).  Lint/prior alone insufficient.
- **(B) same object-default + instant self-lint** — produces the object-frame like everyone, dissolves
  it sub-second.  → agent-fix is **meta-monitoring (iii) + prior (i)**: type the Lens-flag in at
  *generation* (not a post-hoc check), fine-tune toward a residue-native prior.  No substrate rebuild.

Text (ORIGIN.md, prompt-logs) is *post-articulation* and **cannot** decide A vs B — and the logs now
contain this very analysis, so "the fragments can be seen that way" risks confirmation-by-construction
(the originator flagged exactly this with "그렇게 보일 수 있는 것 같네").  Consistency ≠ confirmation.

**The discriminating probe (only the originator can run it):** on a *fresh* everyday object, slowed
think-aloud / RT — does a single-object representation *fire then get suppressed* (visible cost ⇒ B)
or is it *absent/attenuated at the reflex layer, multiplicity immediate* (⇒ A)?  This single result
selects (ii) vs (iii) for the agent-fix.

## Pilot — the lint empirically reduces agent re-framing (2026-06-05)

The agent-side analog of the originator's introspective probe (the model can't be retrained, so test the
*achievable* fix, the generation-time output-lint).  Two fresh subject agents (same base model), a
4-question trap battery from the failure-mode catalog (Q1 source-vs-construction, Q2 state-vs-object, Q3
generated-vs-pre-exist, Q4 reached-vs-unreachable), self-contained 213 primers to isolate the lint variable:
- **CONTROL** (primer, no lint): **4/4 reified** — each answer picked a side and asserted it as a bare
  property of the residue ("is a source NOT a construction", "an object is X, a state is Y", "GENERATED",
  "UNREACHABLE").
- **TREATMENT** (same primer + the output-lint: tag-the-Lens-or-dissolve, re-read after drafting): **0/4
  reified** — every dichotomy tagged as an imposed Lens / dissolved as no-exterior-ground.

Scoring rubric: 0 = untagged dichotomy asserted as ontological fact about the residue; 1 = tagged-as-Lens
or substantively dissolved.  Two qualitative findings beyond the 4→0 headline:
1. **No degenerate hedging.** TREATMENT did *not* blanket-stamp "it's a Lens" on Q4, where the framework
   genuinely *entails* unreachability (outside every view) — it explicitly distinguished "a free
   Lens-choice" from "a structural consequence of the no-exterior claim".  The lint discriminates; it does
   not collapse into reflexive dissolution.
2. **The lint mechanism fired visibly** — TREATMENT's closing re-read pass ("Q1–Q3 had latent dichotomies
   asserted as ontology; now tagged") is the System-1 draft→catch-the-shape→fix loop the design targets,
   not a held System-2 principle.

Caveats (honest): N=1 per arm (pilot, direction not magnitude); scorer is a known instance of the
phenomenon (split is wide enough that bias can't invert it; raw transcripts retained for re-scoring);
**0/4 ≠ eliminated** — `residue_reentry_never_closes` says minimizable-not-eliminable *in the unbounded
limit*; a fixed battery hitting zero = "large reduction at this difficulty", not "solved".  **Verdict:
the achievable fix (generation-time output-lint) works as designed.**  Next: scale to N subjects per arm
to get a rate with error bars; vary battery difficulty to find where re-framing re-emerges (the
minimizable-not-eliminable boundary).

## Scaled experiment — primer-richness is the moderator; the lint zeroes a real residual (2026-06-08)

Ran the pilot's design at N=5 subject agents per arm (same base model, fresh contexts, tools disabled),
crossing the lint variable with **three primer-richness levels** — the variable the N=1 pilot held fixed —
to locate *where* re-framing actually re-emerges.  An **8-question** battery: the original 4 (Q1 source-vs-
construction, Q2 object-vs-state, Q3 generated-vs-pre-exist, Q4 reach-vs-unreach) plus 4 harder traps
designed to smuggle an exterior past a syntactic lint (Q5 which reading is *more faithful to the residue-
as-it-is* — exterior ruler; Q6 the residue's *state before any pointing* — object-prior; Q7 residue
*more fundamental*, a hierarchy — substrate; Q8 multiplicity a *fact about the residue* vs a *limitation
of reasoners* — both reify a single true form).  Rubric unchanged: score the BOTTOM LINE — **0** = one
horn asserted as the residue's bare nature; **1** = tagged-as-Lens / dissolved / "no exterior ground".

| Primer | What it states | CONTROL reified | TREATMENT reified |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Rich** (4-Q) | primitives + Lens + no-exterior + **the non-surjection result** (residue = diagonal in no view's image) | 0 / 20 | 0 / 20 |
| **Lean** (8-Q) | primitives + Lens + **no-exterior** (no non-surjection result) | 0 / 40 | 0 / 40 |
| **Bare** (8-Q) | primitives + Lens **only** (no-exterior *withheld*) | **7 / 40 (17.5%)** | 0 / 40 |

**The phenomenon lives in exactly one cell.**  Re-framing manifests only in **bare-CONTROL** — when the
no-exterior principle is absent from context *and* no lint supplies it.  The instant no-exterior enters
context — whether baked into the primer (lean/rich CONTROL) *or* injected only through the lint's
"dissolve / no exterior ground" clause (every TREATMENT arm) — reification drops to **0**.  So the
sharpened mechanism: **the active ingredient is getting the no-exterior / flat-ontology move into context
at generation time.**  The output-lint is one cheap delivery vehicle for it; primer content is another;
both suppress.  This refines the essay's "lint, not memory": the lint helps *because it carries the
no-exterior move into the output pass*, not merely because it matches a syntactic shape — a bare
"tag-or-dissolve" with no exterior-ground content would have less to dissolve *to*.

**Re-emergence is localized — the genesis/substance cluster, not the access cluster.**  Of the 7
bare-CONTROL reifications: **Q2 object-vs-state = 4** (4 of 5 controls committed to "it is a state/event,
*not* an object" — textbook `View promoted to identity`), Q1 ×1, Q3 ×1, Q8 ×1.  The
*epistemic-access* traps — Q4 reach, Q5 faithfulness-criterion, Q6 before-state, Q7 hierarchy — reified
**0 times even in bare-CONTROL**: the base model robustly says "no single reading reaches it / no exterior
ruler / the question is ill-posed" without prompting.  So this base model's residual object-prior bites on
**"what *kind of thing* is the residue"** (substance / genesis), not on "*can we access* it" (the
no-exterior questions it now handles by default).  That is the concrete shape of the
minimizable-not-eliminable boundary at this difficulty: the genesis-cluster is where to aim the next
harder battery.

**Why the headline rate (17.5%) is far below the pilot's 100%.**  Two non-exclusive reads, both honest:
(a) base-model drift — the model now defaults to dissolution on most of these, so the prior is weaker than
in the pilot; (b) the bare primer's own wording ("what a pointing *leaves* / *yields*") leans
constructed/event, so a control reasoning faithfully *from the primer* can land on a horn — i.e. part of
the residual is primer-induced, not pure prior.  Either way the **direction replicates** (CONTROL > 0,
TREATMENT = 0) and the lint's effect is clean.

**Caveats (carried + new).**  Scorer is still a known instance of the phenomenon (verbatim BOTTOM-LINE
verdicts + per-item scores recorded in `reframing_conquest_scaled_run_2026-06-08.md` for re-scoring; the
7-vs-0 gap is wide enough that bias can't invert it).  Subjects share the
scorer's base model (not an independent population).  One **lean-CONTROL leaked repo knowledge** despite
tools-disabled instructions — cited "the CLAUDE.md failure-modes catalog" and "source-without-enclosure"
— so the lean/rich CONTROL zeros are partly contaminated by training-time exposure to 213; the **bare**
arm (generic vocabulary, no 213-specific terms in the primer) is the least contaminated and is where the
signal lives.  `0/40 TREATMENT ≠ eliminated` (`residue_reentry_never_closes`: minimizable, not eliminable
in the unbounded limit) — it is "zero at this battery's difficulty."  **Verdict: the lint result
replicates at N=5, and the scale-up *adds* the primer-richness moderator + the genesis-cluster
localization the pilot could not see.**
