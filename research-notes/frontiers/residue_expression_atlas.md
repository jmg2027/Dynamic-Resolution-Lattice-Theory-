# Residue-expression atlas — the residue is expressed multi-directionally (OPEN)

**Frontier, not a closed classification.**  "How do you express the essential residue?" is *not*
one phenomenon (Cantor non-surjection / the diagonal).  The residue is given form across the repo by
**structurally distinct mechanisms**, and along **several orthogonal directions at once**.  Two
recurring failure modes here, both to be resisted:

- **collapse to one axis** — "it's all the Cantor diagonal" / "two poles (over/under-determination)".
  Both an Explore sweep *and* the author committed this; it is `view promoted to identity`.
- **flee to no structure** — "seven separate things, nothing connects."  Also wrong: there is an
  organizing spine (`the_form_of_the_residue` four faces) and a single generator (`P`).

## The cohomology is the multi-directional apparatus (the K_{3,2}^{(c=2)} + Δ⁴ answer)

The heavy cohomology exists because residue-expression is **inherently graded** — one residue (`P`,
the `(2,1,3)` atom) expressed along three orthogonal indices, all generated by the *same* `P`
(`synthesis_interlock_map`, `k32_cohomology_simplex_higher_insight`):

- **degree** `k` — the cup-ladder `H^k → α^{k+1}` (`CupLadderUniversalK`); a `+1` graduation per
  degree.  **Terminates** at the `d = 5` skeleton (cohomological content closed at `k = 1,2`).
- **multiplicity** `c` — `c` independent parallel layers, `c` independent `H²` ψ-discriminators
  (`EnrichedKNSNTc.parametric_c_independent_h2_classes`); breadth, not depth.
- **face** — the `Δ⁴` sub-faces `C(5,k)` (`Simplex/Counts`), the spatial skeleton; `ω` the unique
  `Sym(3)`-invariant 2-cocycle is itself a **fixed point** inside the cohomology (`Sq⁰ ω = ω`).

This is the **finite** apparatus.  Against it stands the **infinite** apparatus — the unbounded
ascent / νF escape / non-surjection.  Verified: **zero Lean cross-references** between
`Lib/Physics/AlphaEM`∪`Lib/Math/Cohomology` and `FlatOntologyClosure`∪`CoResidue`∪odometer.  The two
halves were prose-only connected.
