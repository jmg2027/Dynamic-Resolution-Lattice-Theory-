| **Residue** (the diagonal, reached-by-none) | 0 sections | **wall (0)** |

part, the `L`-parameters = the tag-`many` (free) part, the `Residue` = the tag-`0` (wall) part, absence =
tag-`∅`.  So the calculus classifies its **own components** by the same four-way tag it applies to every
object.  This is the reflexive closure of the seminar: not only is the master classifier the wall
(R4), but the normal form itself is a tetrachotomy reading.
