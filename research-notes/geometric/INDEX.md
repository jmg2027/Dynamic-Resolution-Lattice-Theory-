## The generating space (the dials)

The construction "every relation of two objects is itself an object, recurse"
has a choice at each step.  The atlas is the product of these dials; each
setting is a reading of the slash:

1. **Directedness** вЂ” undirected (`a/b = b/a`, the axiom symmetry В§3.3) vs
   directed (the difference-Lens sign, В§6.7).
2. **Quotient amount** вЂ” how much of prim-distinctness is honored: full quotient
   (new object identified with a combination of its parents) в†’ collapse; free
   (new object = independent direction) в†’ dimension growth; algebraic (new
   object distinct but `P`-related) в†’ Stern-Brocot.
3. **Combining map** вЂ” the geometric/algebraic form of "relation": arithmetic
   mean `(a+b)/2`; mediant `(p+r)/(q+s)`; orthogonal adjunction; off-segment
   complex map `x+(yв€’x)w`; geometric mean; other MГ¶bius/`SL(2,в„¤)` maps.
4. **Growth rule** вЂ” which pairs spawn: adjacent only (dyadic / Stern-Brocot)
   vs all pairs (complete) vs directed pairs.
5. **Readout invariant** вЂ” what you measure of the resulting object: positions,
   depth / ruler, angles, volume, the generating matrix's trace/det/disc,
   spectrum, dimension.
